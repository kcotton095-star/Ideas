1. What the service does

Ascent Solutions Group answers after-hours calls on behalf of a client business, collects the caller's details, records the nature of the issue, logs the call, and delivers a structured call summary to the client. The client remains solely responsible for dispatch decisions, technician scheduling, diagnosis, pricing, and all service delivery to the caller.

2. Not an emergency service

Ascent is not an emergency service and is not a substitute for 911. Callers reporting gas odors, smoke, fire, carbon monoxide, or any life-safety hazard are instructed to contact 911 or the appropriate utility immediately.

3. Fees and term

Service is billed monthly according to the plan selected by the client, plus any one-time setup fee stated at signup. Plans are month-to-month with no long-term contract, and may be cancelled in line with the client's service agreement. The client's signed service agreement controls in the event of any conflict with this page.
