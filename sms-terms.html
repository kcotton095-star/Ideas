SMS Terms & Conditions

Last updated: July 14, 2026

Consent to receive text messages from Ascent Solutions Group is entirely voluntary. It is not a condition of purchasing any goods or services, of opening an account, or of completing any transaction. Clients who do not opt in receive the same service and the same call alerts by email.

1. Program description

The Ascent Solutions Group After-Hours Call Alerts program sends text messages to business owners and on-call contacts at companies that use our after-hours answering service. Messages contain summaries of calls we answered on the client's behalf, and occasional account or service notices. We do not send marketing or promotional text messages under this program, and we do not text members of the general public.

2. How to opt in

Opt-in is a separate, standalone action. A client checks an unchecked consent box on our SMS opt-in page, or replies START to a message from us. Opting in is never bundled with signing up for, paying for, or continuing the answering service.

3. Message frequency

Message frequency varies. In general you receive approximately one message for each after-hours call we answer for your business.

4. Cost

Ascent Solutions Group does not charge for these messages. Message and data rates may apply, depending on your mobile carrier and plan. Contact your carrier for details about your plan.

5. How to opt out

Reply STOP to any message at any time. You will receive a single confirmation message and no further messages to that number. Opting out of SMS does not cancel, suspend, downgrade, or otherwise change your answering service — call alerts simply continue by email only. To resume texts, reply START or contact us.

6. Help

Reply HELP to any message, call (843) 640-5084, or email hello@ascentsolutionsgroup.org.

7. Supported carriers and delivery

Message delivery is subject to carrier availability. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

8. Privacy

Mobile numbers collected for this program are used only to deliver call alerts and service notices for your account. We do not sell mobile numbers, and we do not share them with third parties for their own marketing. Mobile information is shared only with the messaging providers we use to deliver the messages on our behalf, and with no one else. See our Privacy Policy.

9. Changes

We may update these SMS Terms. The current version is always posted at this URL.
