SMS Messaging & Communications Compliance

Transparency about how text message alerts work within our service

SMS Opt-In & Consent

SMS is optional. Every client receives after-hours call summaries by email. Clients who also want summaries by text opt in separately on our SMS opt-in page by checking a box that is unchecked by default. Consent to receive SMS is not a condition of purchasing, starting, or continuing our service, and is not required to complete any transaction — clients who do not opt in receive the same service at the same price. You may withdraw consent at any time by replying STOP, with no effect on your answering service.

Consent to receive SMS is not a condition of purchasing our service. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message or by contacting us directly.

Message Frequency & Rates

  • Message frequency varies based on your call volume. You will receive one SMS alert per handled call, plus any scheduled weekly reports you have enabled.
  • Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan.
  • Ascent Solutions Group is not responsible for charges imposed by your wireless carrier.
  • For questions about your carrier's rates, contact your mobile provider directly.

How to Opt Out (STOP)

To stop receiving SMS notifications at any time, reply STOP to any message we send. You will receive a one-time confirmation that your opt-out request has been processed. After that, no further messages will be sent to that number unless you re-enroll.

To re-enroll, text START to our messaging number or contact us at hello@ascentsolutionsgroup.org.
